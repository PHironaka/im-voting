It starts with awareness. People don’t even know midterms even exist, let alone why they’re important.

Civic engagement has to be a cultural movement. Not something forced upon people — that’s why celebrity talking head PSAs are usually pretty ineffective. You have to show not tell. People didn’t protest until they saw their friends participate in the Women’s March. When Sara Bareilles posted a video of herself calling Congress for the first time, it inspired others to do the same. Karlie Kloss has done the same to protest child separation at the border. And Taylor Swift has been taking her followers along on her journey.

There is always talk about the “I voted” sticker, but by the time you see the sticker, it’s a wrap — no one is running down to the polls to cast their vote.
